ROBOLUTION

A. Eligibility

  1. All Erovoutika partner schools, whether high school or university, are eligible to compete.
  2. The university, college, or school may be represented by no more than one (1) person.
  3. Representative of the college, institution, or school:
    1. Must be a legitimate high school student attending the college, university, or other educational institution that they represent.
    2. Has to be officially enrolled for the 2024-2025 Academic Year
    3. Must possess good moral standing and good character.
  4. A Certificate of Endorsement from the principle, dean, or chair must be submitted by the school, college, or university with the participating student's registration or enrollment form.
  5. Participants who meet the requirements must complete their registration using the Google Forms link.

B.Score and Pointing System

    The distribution of the quiz bee's scope is as follows, and the panel of judges will determine it.

C. Manner of Answering

  1. Every participant is required to provide their own white board, board eraser and marker.
  2. The Quiz Master will read each question twice.
  3. After the question has been read twice, the time restriction begins on the Quiz Master's cue. Quizzers can only write anything on their white boards during that period.
  4. The final responses ought to be enclosed in a circle or rectangle and written in a legible manner. Responses that do not adhere to this guideline will not be approved.
  5. The first response will be taken into consideration if there are two (2) replies written on the board, each surrounded by a circle or rectangle.
  6. In order to prevent any changes being made to the Final Answer, watchers will be allocated to each quizzer's column.
  7. Upon the expiration of the allotted time, the quiz master will call on the participants to elevate their boards and declare the ultimate solution. As the Panel of Judges and the Quiz Master record the points, quiz takers who correctly answered will continue to stand with their boards raised. Note: The Panel of Judges retains the authority to amend Item
  8. The final answer will be deemed incorrect if spelling is incorrect. 9. Quizzers who provide an erroneous or blank response will not lose points.
  9. Quizzers who provide incorrect or no answers will not lose any points.
  10. It is completely forbidden to use reference books, e-books, notebooks, cell phones, or any other materials or devices that may contain information while the competition is being held. If the aforementioned regulation is broken, the participant will be deemed forfeited.
  11. If there is a tie, the Clincher round will start. The panel of judges will select the question for this round, which may be objective or analytical in nature. The Clincher round follows the same guidelines as the Easy, Moderate, and Difficult rounds and goes on until the tie is broken.

D. Panel of Judges

  1. Three (3) members plus the chair make up the Panel of Judges.
  2. None of the quizzers shall be affiliated with any member of the Panel.

E. Clarifications

  1. The only person who can ask questions about the question or answer that is provided at a given time is the quizzer.
  2. Any questions should be directed to the panel of judges, and issues should only be brought up and answered within the time allocated for each inquiry.
  3. Observe civility and appropriate behaviour when asking questions. Any rude or disparaging behaviour during the competition will be punished appropriately, as determined by Erovoutika. This could result in the offending party being disqualified from the E-Robo Quiz Bee or being barred from taking part in any of Erovoutika's next quiz bees.
  4. The judges' decision is final and non-reversible.
  5. To improve the quiz competition's administration even further, Erovoutika is able to amend any portion of the rules and guidelines.

E. Miscellaneous

  1. The participating institution's registration fee, lodging costs, transportation costs, and food allowance are entirely the responsibility of the represented school, college, or university.
